Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. Here's exactly what we collect and why.

1. Who We Are

CC-AI is a free AI assistant created by Chorm Chhaiya (Yaxy), a student at Tepranom High School in Cambodia. This app is a personal project built to help people with coding, learning, and conversation.

2. What Data We Collect

  • Your name — only if you choose to enter it in your profile (stored on your device)
  • Usage statistics — number of chats and images generated (anonymous, stored in Firebase)
  • Last seen time — when you last used the app (anonymous)
  • Chat history — stored ONLY on your device (localStorage), never on our servers

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ We do NOT collect your email address
  • ❌ We do NOT collect your phone number
  • ❌ We do NOT sell your data to anyone
  • ❌ We do NOT store your chat messages on our servers
  • ❌ We do NOT track your location
  • ❌ We do NOT show personalized ads

4. Third-Party Services

CC-AI uses these third-party services to function:

  • Groq AI — processes your chat messages to generate responses. See Groq's privacy policy at groq.com
  • Cloudflare AI — used for image generation. See Cloudflare's privacy policy at cloudflare.com
  • Firebase (Google) — stores anonymous usage statistics. See Google's privacy policy at google.com
  • ElevenLabs — used for voice responses. See ElevenLabs' policy at elevenlabs.io

5. Your Messages

When you send a message to CC-AI, it is sent to Groq's servers to generate a response. We do not store these messages. Please do not share sensitive personal information like passwords, credit card numbers, or ID numbers in the chat.

6. Cookies & Local Storage

CC-AI uses your browser's localStorage to save your profile settings and chat history. This data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers. You can clear this anytime through your browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

CC-AI is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has shared personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. We will notify users by updating the date at the top of this page.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact us on TikTok: @unluckyguy0001
