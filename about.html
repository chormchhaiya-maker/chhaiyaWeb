🤖 What is CC-AI?

CC-AI is a free AI assistant that anyone can use without signing up. It's powered by state-of-the-art AI models and designed to be helpful, honest, and fun to talk to.

CC-AI speaks Khmer 🇰🇭 and English, making it one of the few AI assistants that truly supports the Cambodian language.

💬Smart conversation in Khmer & English
🎨AI image generation
🖥️Expert coding help
🎵Song lyrics writing
🎤Voice input & output
📲Works as a mobile app
🌐Translation tool
💾Chat history saved

🔒 Is CC-AI Safe?

Yes! CC-AI is designed with safety in mind:

✅ We never store your chat messages on our servers

✅ Your chat history stays on your device only

✅ We never sell your data

✅ No account or email required to use CC-AI

✅ CC-AI refuses to generate harmful or illegal content
